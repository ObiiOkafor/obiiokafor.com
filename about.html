About Obii

Product leader, speaker, author and builder.

Obiageli (Obii) Okafor is a London-based Senior Product Manager, keynote speaker, author and technology leader helping organisations build products that solve meaningful problems while creating more inclusive pathways into technology.

At a glance

  • Based: London, United Kingdom
  • Current role: Senior Product Manager at 8x8, Chief Executive & Co-Founder of PMHelp, and Co-Founder of Capstone
  • Speaking: Product management, AI, leadership, career growth and visibility
  • Author: Impossible to Ignore

Product Leadership

Building products that solve meaningful problems.

Obii is currently a Senior Product Manager at 8x8, where she leads strategic product initiatives across cloud communications, AI-powered experiences and enterprise platforms used by global partners and customers.

Throughout her career, she has delivered products across telecommunications, fintech, artificial intelligence, SaaS and digital transformation, working with startups and multinational organisations to build products used by millions of users worldwide.

Access Into Tech

Creating pathways for people to grow.

Alongside her corporate career, Obii is the Chief Executive and Co-Founder of PMHelp, a global platform helping people build successful careers in product management through education, mentoring, career programmes, AI-powered tools and industry initiatives.

She is also the Co-Founder of Capstone, an AI-powered platform that helps professionals gain real-world experience through practical simulations and project-based learning.

Speaking And Ideas

Helping people build visible, lasting impact.

Obii is a sought-after international speaker and has delivered keynote presentations, workshops and panel discussions for global technology companies, universities and industry conferences.

Her talks explore product management, artificial intelligence, career growth, leadership, digital transformation and personal visibility, helping professionals build careers that create lasting impact.

Beyond The Bio

Commercial product leadership with a deep sense of purpose.

Originally from Nigeria and now based in London, Obii combines commercial product leadership with a passion for education, mentorship and innovation.

She is the author of Impossible to Ignore, a practical guide to building a career through visibility, credibility and consistent value creation.
